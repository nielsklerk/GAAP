\chapter{Discussion}

A disadvantage of this method compared to a method such as model-fitting is that it cannot compensate for bad image quality or oversaturated objects. Model-fitting methods can use the surrounding good pixels to estimate the flux of the object, but \gls{gaap} cannot do that and will thereby get a really large error on the flux as the RMS map has large (\textbf{VALUES}) values at those spots. 
For the noise-limited observations, GAAP does not improve results much
compared to other methods such as EXMAPLES as the error on the color mea-
surements is dominated by the noise in the image and not the difference in the
PSF.
